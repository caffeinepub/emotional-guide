**Goal:** Convert resource links to flip-card images with pop artist content while preserving mood dropdown and fixing voice conversation.

**Planned changes:**
- Keep "How are you feeling?" mood dropdown unchanged in ConversationInput
- Convert food, music, stories, self-care, and videos links to clickable image cards in cloud layout
